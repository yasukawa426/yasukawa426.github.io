Weird Girl Dream

Score: 7/10

Date: 26/09/2024

It started in a facility inside a treehouse. A lot of people lived there, kind of like a big house. I was with my friend João, and we decided to hang out and have lunch together. As we were leaving the treehouse, a woman spoke to me. I think she worked there, maybe in a shop or canteen; I’m not sure. The only thing I remember about her appearance is that she was blonde, with shoulder-length hair. She was very pretty. We had a short exchange, and I remember her laughing. Then my friend and I left.

For three straight days, we just hung out, walked the streets, talked, and had lunch. It was a really good time. Each time we left the treehouse, the woman would talk to me. We really hit it off, and I was starting to like her.

On the third day, she decided to join us for lunch. It was nice.

Out of nowhere, on the fourth day, the scene changed. At the time, it made sense. Now we were in one of my relatives' houses in another city, and it was nighttime—just me and her in the kitchen 1.

Relatives' House Layout

The house was dark. She was leaning into the kitchen sink. We started talking.

Girl: I really like you, you smell like a good person. You’ve never gotten mad at me, never yelled at me, never hurt me in any way. I know I smell really bad because of sweat, and you didn’t even say anything. You’re so nice!

For some reason, she started to really creep me out, so I began to walk backwards.

Me: There was literally nothing I could have done. Literally nothing... What could I have done?

She smirked, giving me chills, and started walking towards me.

Girl: There were a lot of things you could have done.

At that moment, I was really scared. I took off running down Hallway 1, and she chased after me, fast. Suddenly, I started hearing the voices of my relatives all screaming at me at once—around ten voices shouting, “Come this way! Here! Come here!”

Then I saw their initials flying around, showing me where they were, like a video game objective. Unfortunately, I was heading in the opposite direction. I turned back, rushing down Hallway 1 again, shoving the girl out of the way—it was terrifying. I ran into one of the bedrooms and saw one of my relatives calling me. I got close to them but was confused about what to do. Next thing I knew, I was in the living room.

The girl wasn’t following me anymore, but there were a lot of unknown people acting very strangely, almost like zombies. I started jumping on the sofas to avoid them. Again, I noticed a symbol: a square filled with dots in a grid pattern. I knew exactly what I had to do. I screamed as loudly and as long as I could, completely emptying my lungs.

Once I finished, all the “zombies” began to walk away slowly and leave the house. Suddenly, I woke up, feeling scared and confused.

The End.

Review

It was a good dream, solid 7/10. The first part was very nice. I had a lot of fun hanging out with my friend and the woman. The second part, though, was very scary and weird, and I didn't like the way I suddenly woke up.
