Uber Dream

Score: 10/10

Date: 29/07/2025

Disclaimer: I decided to try and add some thing to make the reading more enjoayble/immersive. Sorry if its cringe.

It all started with me either being at my old house (the one I lived in when I was a kid) waiting to go to school, or already being at school. I decided to order a pizza. However, after I paid, I realized I had put in the wrong address. I managed to fix it, no problem, but when the pizza arrived, it actually turned into an Uber bike ride. (Not sure if you guys have that outside of Brazil. It's basically an Uber ride on a motorcycle instead of a car. You ride on the back, holding onto the driver).

It started taking me home (So if I wasn’t already at school, I suddenly teleported there. Dream logic, yk.). It was extremely fast. Like, sound-speed fast (I was watching a Sonic video when I fell asleep, lmao). I was holding my phone with just my thumb and index finger, gripping it with all my strength but barely managing to keep it. At one point, I realized there was no way I’d be able to hold on and ended up letting it go. When the bike finally stopped, I realized it wasn’t my phone that I lost. It had actually been in my pocket the whole time.

After that, while we were riding, another Uber bike showed up next to us. The driver and the passenger started talking to us, super casual, my driver and them just started a conversation. They were so close to us that we almost looked like a car. I was very anxious due to the weird situation. After that, we approached the "Death curve" ("Curva da Morte" - It's a well known road in my old neighborhood). While doing it I was holding onto my driver's backpack which he was wearing, but he was only using one of the straps, so I fell off the bike along with his backpack. Surprisingly, I landed on my feet, running, because of the momentum. The driver stopped and came back to me, noticed that my feet were all dirty with mud, and looked very disappointed.

He got off the bike and for some reason I started driving it (I think. I'm not sure exactly when I started driving, but I eventually did). At this point, the Uber driver seemed uninterested in taking me home. We started going to weird dangerous areas (crime-wise). It was a long journey. I remember going to a bunch of places, dodging traffic, pushing the bike on foot, stuff like that. At one point we stopped and went inside this bar. It was full of goth Corintianas (Women who are into Corinthians, the soccer team). I even said something like "I am going to come back here with my friends later". Me and the Uber driver started to become friends.

We were almost at my place, and for some reason we were in this square on foot. It looked sketchy and was full of people. Suddenly some random person took the lollipop they were sucking and rubbed it on my arm. The Uber driver said it was Molly (the drug) and started freaking out, saying that I was gonna get super high and stuff. I told him not to worry, that it only kicks in after 30 minutes, and we were really close to my house. He calmed down and started leading me somewhere.

While we were walking I started to get high. Colors were more vivid. I was having fun and felt happier. I felt very good. He took me to his community and started showing me around and introducing me to people. He seemed like a big deal there and looked like he knew everyone. Then he introduced me to this woman and her daughter. Apparently she had depression and they wanted me to become friends with her. The last thing I remember was me talking to her.

The End.

Review

Overall, it was a super fun dream. Felt like a long journey. I met a lot people and went a lot of places. When I woke up I wished it lasted longer. 10/10. Would dream it again.
